The variable `expected_avg_profit` is a Series where each value represents the estimated average profit per transaction for a customer, as predicted by the Gamma-Gamma model. In other words, it’s the model’s best estimate of what a customer’s typical transaction will yield (in terms of profit or revenue) in the future, based on their historical spending behavior. This value is used later to compute overall expected revenue (and thus CLV) by multiplying it with the predicted number of future transactions from the BG/NBD model.
