When you split your data into 5 equally sized categories, it's called **quintiles**. 

### Definitions:
- **Quantiles**: General term for dividing data into equal-sized intervals.
- **Quintiles**: Splitting data into 5 equal parts.
- **Deciles**: Splitting data into 10 equal parts.
- **Quartiles**: Splitting data into 4 equal parts.

In your case, since you have 5 categories, you've created quintiles. You can still use the same plotting method as before; just adjust the code to handle your 5 quintiles.
