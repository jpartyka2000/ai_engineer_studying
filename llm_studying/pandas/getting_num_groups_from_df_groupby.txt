Here are three quick ways to find out how many groups your `groupby` produces:

```python
# 1) Use the .ngroups attribute
gb = df.groupby(['colA','colB'])
print(gb.ngroups)

# 2) Take the length of the groupby object
print(len(gb))

# 3) Look at the .groups dict
print(len(gb.groups))
```

All three will give you the same integer: the number of distinct `(colA, colB)` groups in your DataFrame.
