### Alternatives

Any of these will work to get the lone value out of that 1-element Series:

* `grp["SUBMITTED_APPLICATION_day_7"].iloc[0]`
* `grp["SUBMITTED_APPLICATION_day_7"].iat[0]`
* `grp["SUBMITTED_APPLICATION_day_7"].values[0]`
* `grp["SUBMITTED_APPLICATION_day_7"].item()`  (only if you’re sure it’s exactly one element)

Then you can compare `x == 0` as a simple boolean and proceed.
