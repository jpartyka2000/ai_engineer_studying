After converting your one-column Spark DataFrame to a Pandas DataFrame, you can convert it to a Pandas Series using the `.squeeze()` method. For example:

```python
y_train_pd = preprocessed_data.y_train.toPandas().squeeze()
```

If the resulting DataFrame has only one column, `.squeeze()` will convert it to a Series. Alternatively, you could use indexing:

```python
y_train_pd = preprocessed_data.y_train.toPandas().iloc[:, 0]
```

Both approaches will give you a Pandas Series containing the values from your original one-column Spark DataFrame.
