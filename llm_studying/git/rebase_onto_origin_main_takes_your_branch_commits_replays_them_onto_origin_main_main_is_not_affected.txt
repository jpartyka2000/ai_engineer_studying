Correct.

"Rebase onto `origin/main`" means you're taking *your branch's* commits and replaying them on top of `origin/main`. 

- `origin/main` is unchanged — it's just the foundation you're building on
- Your local branch gets rewritten with new commits
- After you force push, your remote branch (`origin/jpartyka/port_old_eval_agent`) gets updated

At no point does `main` or `origin/main` get modified. You're only changing your own branch.
