Not quite – an “episode” is whatever your env defines it to be. In the vanilla Gym/API sense, an episode is:

1. You call `obs, _ = env.reset()`
2. You loop

   * Pick an action
   * Call `env.step(action)`
   * Until the env returns `terminated=True` or `truncated=True`

In your `QnAEnv` as written, `step(...)` always sets `terminated=True` immediately after one question, so **each** evaluation episode only ever covers **one** question.

If you wanted an episode to span *all* of the questions in your dataset, you’d need to change your logic so that `terminated` doesn’t flip to `True` until you’ve looped through (and answered) every question you care about.
