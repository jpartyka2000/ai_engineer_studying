## Trajectory Turn Explanation

A **trajectory turn** is one **step or exchange** in a conversation/interaction sequence between users, AI assistants, and tools.

## Breaking Down the Turn Types:

```python
# A "trajectory" is a sequence of turns in order:
trajectory = [
    Turn 1: user → "What's the weather in NYC?"
    Turn 2: assistant → "Let me check that for you"
    Turn 3: tool_input → {"function": "get_weather", "params": {"city": "NYC"}}
    Turn 4: tool_output → {"temp": 72, "conditions": "sunny"}
    Turn 5: assistant → "It's 72°F and sunny in NYC"
]
```

## Each Turn Represents:

```python
class TrajectoryCitation(BaseModel):
    turn_index: int  # Position in conversation (0, 1, 2, ...)
    turn_type: Literal["user", "assistant", "tool_input", "tool_output"]
    snippet: Optional[str]  # Brief excerpt of what was said/done

# Example citation pointing to specific turn:
citation = TrajectoryCitation(
    turn_index=3,  # 4th turn (zero-based)
    turn_type="tool_output",
    snippet="temp: 72, conditions: sunny"
)
```

## Complete Example Trajectory:

```python
# Full conversation trajectory with citations:
trajectory = [
    # Turn 0
    {"turn_type": "user", "content": "Calculate the tax on $1000 at 8%"},
    
    # Turn 1
    {"turn_type": "assistant", "content": "I'll calculate that tax for you"},
    
    # Turn 2
    {"turn_type": "tool_input", "content": "calculate(1000 * 0.08)"},
    
    # Turn 3
    {"turn_type": "tool_output", "content": "80"},
    
    # Turn 4
    {"turn_type": "assistant", "content": "The tax on $1000 at 8% is $80"}
]

# Citation referencing where the calculation came from:
citation = TrajectoryCitation(
    turn_index=3,  # Points to the tool_output turn
    turn_type="tool_output",
    snippet="80"
)
```

## Why Track Trajectory Turns?

1. **Evaluation** - Analyze each step of AI reasoning
2. **Attribution** - Know where information came from
3. **Debugging** - Identify which turn went wrong
4. **Citations** - Reference specific moments in the interaction

**Bottom line**: A trajectory turn is a **single interaction step** in a conversation flow - like one message, one tool call, or one response. The citation system lets you point back to specific turns to show where information or decisions originated.
