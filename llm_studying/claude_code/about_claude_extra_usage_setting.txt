The **Extra Usage** setting is a feature available to Claude Pro subscribers that allows you to continue using Claude beyond your standard monthly message limit by paying for additional usage.

Here's what I know about it:

- **What it does:** When enabled, Extra Usage lets you keep chatting with Claude after you've used up your included Pro allowance, rather than being cut off until the next billing cycle.

- **How it works:** You can set a spending limit (a maximum dollar amount you're willing to spend on extra usage each month) so you stay in control of costs.

- **Where to find it:** The setting is typically found in your account or subscription settings within Claude.ai.
