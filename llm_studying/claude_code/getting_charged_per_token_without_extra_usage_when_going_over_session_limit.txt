No, there's no penalty charge. 

**You simply get blocked** — Claude Code stops accepting new prompts until your 5-hour window resets. Your conversation history stays intact, you just can't send messages.

Without extra usage turned on, you **cannot** be charged beyond your subscription fee. The system just cuts you off.

If you *did* enable extra usage, then you'd pay per-token at API rates for anything beyond your allocation — but that's opt-in, not automatic.
