To remove all tab characters in a single file in VSCode, you have a few options:

**Option 1: Find and Replace (Recommended)**
1. Press `Ctrl+H` (Windows/Linux) or `Cmd+H` (Mac) to open Find and Replace
2. In the "Find" field, type `\t` (this represents a tab character)
3. In the "Replace" field, enter your desired replacement (spaces or nothing)
4. Click "Replace All" or press `Ctrl+Alt+Enter` (Windows/Linux) or `Cmd+Option+Enter` (Mac)

**Option 2: Convert Indentation**
If you want to convert tabs to spaces:
1. Open the Command Palette: `Ctrl+Shift+P` (Windows/Linux) or `Cmd+Shift+P` (Mac)
2. Type "Convert Indentation to Spaces"
3. Select it and press Enter

**Option 3: Retab the File**
1. Select all text: `Ctrl+A` (Windows/Linux) or `Cmd+A` (Mac)
2. Open Command Palette: `Ctrl+Shift+P` or `Cmd+Shift+P`
3. Type "Reindent Lines" or "Format Document"

The Find and Replace method (`\t`) is the most flexible if you want to remove tabs entirely or replace them with a specific number of spaces.
