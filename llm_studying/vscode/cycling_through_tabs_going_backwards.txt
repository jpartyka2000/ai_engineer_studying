## Cycling Backwards Through Tabs:

### **Go Backward:**
- **`Ctrl+Shift+Tab`** (Windows/Linux)
- **`Cmd+Shift+Tab`** (Mac)

This cycles through tabs in **reverse order**.

### **Quick Reference:**
- **Forward**: `Ctrl+Tab` 
- **Backward**: `Ctrl+Shift+Tab`

### **Alternative for Tab Switcher:**
When you press `Ctrl+Tab` and **hold Ctrl**:
- Keep pressing `Tab` to go forward
- Press **`Shift+Tab`** to go backward in the list
- Release `Ctrl` to select

### **By Position (Mac):**
- **Next tab**: `Cmd+Option+→`
- **Previous tab**: `Cmd+Option+←`

**Tip**: Think of it like browser tabs - same shortcuts work there too!
