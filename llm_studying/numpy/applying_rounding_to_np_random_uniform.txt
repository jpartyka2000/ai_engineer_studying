You can wrap your call to `np.random.uniform` with `np.round()` and specify 3 as the number of decimal places. For example:

```python
df_campaign_data['conv_rate'] = np.round(np.random.uniform(0.25, 0.5, size=len(df_campaign_data)), 3)
```

This will generate uniform random numbers between 0.25 and 0.5 and then round each value to 3 decimal places before assigning them to the `conv_rate` column.
