There is no built-in toPolars() method in PySpark to directly convert a Spark DataFrame to a Polars DataFrame. However, you can achieve this using an intermediate conversion to Pandas, like so:

import polars as pl

# Assume `spark_df` is your existing Spark DataFrame
pandas_df = spark_df.toPandas()

# Convert to Polars DataFrame
polars_df = pl.from_pandas(pandas_df)

Notes:

This requires all the data to fit into memory, since toPandas() collects the full Spark DataFrame on the driver.

Not suitable for large datasets unless you filter or sample first.
