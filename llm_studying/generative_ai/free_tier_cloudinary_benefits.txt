No, you're not restricted. Cloudinary's free plan fully supports almost all features and can be used without restrictions, even in production, as long as you don't exceed your 30-day free credit allowance.

The key details for the free tier:

- **Uploading (Upload API)**: The Upload API is not rate-limited. You can upload freely.
- **Retrieving**: Images are served via CDN at public URLs — no API call needed, just access the URL directly.
- **Admin API** (listing, deleting resources): Limited to 500 requests per hour on the free plan.
- **File size limit**: Max image file size on the free plan is 10 MB. More than enough for album covers.
- **Credits**: You get 25 credits per month. 1 credit = 1 GB storage or 1 GB bandwidth or 1,000 transformations.
- **No time limit**: The free plan is not time-limited. You can use it for as long as you want.
- **Soft limits**: Cloudinary's limits are soft limits. Once you exceed them, they'll ask you to upgrade. They won't suddenly cut you off.

For your use case of uploading a handful of album covers and passing their URLs to Kontext, the free tier will be more than sufficient. You'd barely scratch the surface of the 25 credits.
