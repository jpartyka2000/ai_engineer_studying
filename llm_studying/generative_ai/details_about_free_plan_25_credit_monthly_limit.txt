No. The free plan is not time-limited. You can use it for as long as you want, provided that your usage does not exceed the free plan's 25 credit limit.

The 25 credits reset every 30 days (it's a rolling monthly allowance, not a one-time trial). So as long as you stay under 25 credits each month — which for your use case of storing and serving a few album cover images is barely anything — your API calls will continue working indefinitely with no disruption.
