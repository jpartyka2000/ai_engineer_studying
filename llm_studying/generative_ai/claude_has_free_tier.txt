Yes, Claude has a free tier. You can use Claude for free at claude.ai or through the Claude mobile app with some usage limits.

For more usage, there's Claude Pro, a paid subscription that offers higher limits and priority access during busy times.

There's also an API for developers, which has its own pricing structure based on usage (tokens processed).
