For album art on your setup, I'd recommend:

## Top pick: SDXL Lightning or SDXL Turbo

- Native 1024x1024 resolution (ideal for album covers)
- 1-4 step generation (very fast)
- ~6GB model size — fits comfortably in 18GB
- Quality nearly matches full SDXL

## Second choice: Full SDXL + good fine-tune

- Slower (20-30 steps) but more control
- Better with LoRAs for specific art styles
- Many album-art-friendly fine-tunes on CivitAI

## Quantization?

Use **FP16** (half precision) — this is standard for diffusion models on Apple Silicon and doesn't noticeably hurt quality. Avoid INT8 quantization for album art; the quality drop is more visible in images than in text.

## Skip these:

- **SD 1.5**: Fast but 512x512 native — you'd need upscaling for album art
- **FLUX**: Excellent quality but the models are 12B+ parameters, tight on 18GB and slower

## Best Mac app for your use case

**Draw Things** (free, App Store) — optimized for Apple Silicon, supports SDXL Lightning, easy to swap models, has upscaling built-in.

## Realistic expectations

On M3 Pro with SDXL Lightning: ~2-5 seconds per image
On M3 Pro with full SDXL: ~15-30 seconds per image

Go with **SDXL Lightning** in **Draw Things** — best quality-to-speed ratio for album covers on your hardware.
