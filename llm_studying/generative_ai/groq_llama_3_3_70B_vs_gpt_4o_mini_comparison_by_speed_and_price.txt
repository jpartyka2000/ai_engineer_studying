- **Choose GPT-4o-mini if:** Cost is your primary concern and you're okay with standard API latency
- **Choose Llama 3.3 70B on Groq if:** Speed matters more than cost (real-time chat, agentic workflows, interactive applications)

The quality between the two is reasonably comparable for most tasks, though they have different strengths. Llama 3.3 70B is a larger model (70B vs ~8B parameters for 4o-mini) and may perform better on complex reasoning, while GPT-4o-mini tends to be better at instruction following and has tighter OpenAI-style guardrails.
