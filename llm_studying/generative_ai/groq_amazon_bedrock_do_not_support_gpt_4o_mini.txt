GPT-4o-mini is an OpenAI model and isn't available on Groq or Amazon Bedrock—those platforms don't have access to OpenAI's models.

**What each platform offers:**

- **Groq** — Runs open models (Llama 3, Mixtral, Gemma, etc.) on their custom LPU hardware, which is extremely fast for inference
- **Amazon Bedrock** — Hosts models from Anthropic (Claude), Meta (Llama), Mistral, Cohere, Amazon (Titan), and others—but not OpenAI
- **OpenAI API** — The only way to access GPT-4o-mini

**Your options:**

1. **Stick with OpenAI's API** if you specifically need GPT-4o-mini
2. **Switch models** if speed is your priority—Groq's Llama 3 70B or Mixtral inference is very fast and might meet your needs depending on the task
3. **Use Azure OpenAI** if you want GPT-4o-mini on different infrastructure (still OpenAI models, but hosted by Microsoft)
