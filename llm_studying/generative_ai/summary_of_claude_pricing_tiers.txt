Here's a summary of Claude's current pricing tiers:

## Claude Pricing Plans

**Free ($0/month)**
- Access to the core AI model for tasks like summarizing articles, drafting emails, and answering complex questions
- Available on the web, iOS, and Android without entering a credit card
- Limited daily usage with message limits that reset every 5 hours, and additional restrictions during peak times
- Includes web search capability

**Pro ($20/month)**
- 5x more usage than the free tier
- Priority access during busy periods, Claude Code for terminal integration, unlimited Projects, and connectors for Google Drive, email, and calendar
- Access to advanced models including Opus
- Early access to new features

**Max ($100 or $200/month)**
- $100/month provides 5x more usage than Pro, and $200/month provides 20x more usage than Pro, with priority access and enhanced performance
- With the $200 tier, Opus-specific caps have been removed, enabling all-day usage for complex tasks
- Highest priority access and earliest access to new models

**Team ($25-30/user/month)**
- With a 5-user minimum, Team plans include all Pro features plus administrative controls and unified billing
- Pooled usage across team members

**Enterprise (custom pricing)**
- Higher usage limits, SSO integration, domain-level admin controls, audit logs, compliance features, and dedicated support

For developers, there's also **API pricing** based on tokens processed, which varies by model (Haiku is cheapest, Opus is most expensive).
