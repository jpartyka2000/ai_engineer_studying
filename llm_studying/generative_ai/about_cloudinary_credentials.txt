Yes. You need three credentials:

- **Cloud name** — identifies your account
- **API key** — public identifier
- **API secret** — private, used to sign requests

You get all three for free when you sign up. They're in your Cloudinary dashboard under Settings > API Keys.

For uploading and managing images programmatically (which is your use case), all three are required. There is one exception — Cloudinary has **unsigned uploads** where you only need the cloud name and an upload preset, but those are designed for client-side/browser uploads and give you less control, so I wouldn't recommend them for your backend workflow.
