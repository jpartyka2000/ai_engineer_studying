Yes, that's correct. When you sign up at console.groq.com, you get access to a Free tier with no credit card required. The Free tier lets you use GroqCloud and call supported models, but your usage is capped by rate limits (requests/tokens per minute or per day).

**Free tier limits:**
- Groq offers 14,400 requests/day on the free tier
- There are also per-minute limits on requests and tokens
- Rate limits apply at the organization level, not individual users. You can hit any limit type depending on which threshold you reach first.

**What happens when you hit limits:**
When you exceed rate limits, the API returns a 429 Too Many Requests HTTP status code. You won't be charged—your requests just get blocked until the limit resets.

**Upgrading options:**
If you're looking for a rate limit increase, the Developer Tier offers up to 10x more than the free tier. It's pay-as-you-go with a credit card. Groq offers a generous Free Tier, a Developer Tier for 10X higher token consumption, and Enterprise plans for custom capacity needs.

**Alternatives if free tier limits are too strict:**
- **OpenRouter** — 30+ free models available
- **Mistral AI** — 1 billion tokens/month on free tier (very generous)
- **Together.ai** — Offers starting credits
- **Google AI Studio** — Still has a usable free tier for Gemini models

If you're doing light development or prototyping, the Groq free tier is usually fine. For heavier usage, Mistral's free tier is notably more generous with volume.
