## The Key Insight

- **Width of the violin = density**: Where it's widest, that's where most samples have SHAP values
- **Color gradient**: Shows whether high (pink) or low (blue) feature values are associated with those SHAP values at that location

So the "rounded blobs" are showing you: "Most of my samples for this feature have SHAP values in this range, with this feature value."
