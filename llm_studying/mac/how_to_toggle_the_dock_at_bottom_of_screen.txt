You can hide the Dock (the bar at the bottom) by pressing **Command + Option + D**. Press the same shortcut again to show it.

Alternatively, you can set it to auto-hide:

1. Go to **System Settings** → **Desktop & Dock**
2. Turn on **Automatically hide and show the Dock**

With auto-hide enabled, the Dock disappears when you're not using it and reappears when you move your cursor to the bottom of the screen.
